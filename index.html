Our story

Founded in 2011 by John Mathew Smith, Konstruct has become the number one construction management firm

Konsctruct's path has not been without problems. Skeptics initially dismissed the projects as mere fantasies that had not stood the test of time. Building codes and budgetary constraints often threatened to stifle our potential. But our exceptional team of architects and engineers never wavered because they believed that the impossible was just a challenge to their ingenuity.

Works

Riverview Plaza

The past year has seen Konstruct successfully completing several landmark projects across various sectors, from commercial and residential buildings to infrastructure and public works. Notable among these achievements is the completion of the iconic Riverview Plaza complex, a monumental mixed-use development set to redefine urban living.

Revitalization of the city center

One of the key upcoming projects is a massive downtown revitalization initiative, which promises to rejuvenate a neglected area and transform it into a thriving urban center. In collaboration with esteemed architects and consultants, Konstruct intends to bring to life a visionary design that reflects the city's unique cultural heritage and offers a vibrant and inclusive space for both residents and visitors.
